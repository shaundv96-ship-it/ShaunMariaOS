# ShaunMariaOS

ShaunMariaOS is a private life operating system designed to help Shaun and Maria manage their journey together — from wedding planning to home ownership, family finances, travel, and future milestones.
## Project Direction

ShaunMariaOS is focused on shared life management for Shaun and Maria.

Included:
- WeddingOS
- FinanceOS
- CalendarOS
- HomeOS
- TravelOS
- FamilyOS in the future

Not included:
- Health tracking
- Career tracking
- Work systems
- Business/order tracking

Those will remain separate projects if needed.
